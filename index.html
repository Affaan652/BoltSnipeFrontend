Questions

Everything you need to know before your first trade.

Is BoltSnipe safe?
+
Your private key is encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption. BoltSnipe never has access to your unencrypted key. You can withdraw your SOL to any external wallet at any time — your funds are always yours.
How do I get started?
+
Open @BoltSnipeBot on Telegram and send /start. A Solana wallet is created for you automatically — no app download, no seed phrase setup, no browser extension. Your wallet address appears on the dashboard immediately. Deposit SOL and you are ready to trade.
Do I need a separate wallet like Phantom?
+
No. BoltSnipe creates a Solana wallet for you automatically on first use. You can also withdraw to Phantom or any other wallet whenever you want.
What tokens can I trade?
+
Any SPL token on Solana that is listed on Jupiter DEX. Paste the contract address and BoltSnipe will fetch the price and route your trade automatically.
What is the fee?
+
1% on every buy and every sell. No monthly fee, no subscription, no hidden charges.
What is the minimum and maximum trade size?
+
Minimum trade is 0.01 SOL. Maximum is 10 SOL per transaction. Keep a small balance for gas — Solana fees are typically around 0.000005 SOL per transaction.
How does Take Profit and Stop Loss work?
+
After buying a token, tap Set TP/SL on the position. Enter your target price (TP) and your floor price (SL). BoltSnipe watches the price around the clock and executes a sell automatically when your level is hit. No need to monitor the chart.
How does the referral program work?
+
Share your unique referral link. Anyone who opens BoltSnipe through your link is your referral. You earn a percentage of their trade fees — 50% in month 1, 30% in month 2, and 25% for life after that. Earnings are tracked automatically and withdrawable as SOL.
What happens if the network is congested?
+
BoltSnipe uses a premium RPC endpoint for fast transaction submission. If a transaction fails, the bot notifies you and your funds remain safe in your wallet.
Can I lose my funds?
+
Trading involves risk. BoltSnipe executes trades as instructed — it does not give financial advice. Use TP/SL to protect your positions. Honeypot detection reduces risk but is not a guarantee against all bad tokens.
Can I use BoltSnipe on mobile?
+
Yes. BoltSnipe works on any device that runs Telegram — mobile, desktop, or web. No installation required.
What if I lose access to my Telegram account?
+
Your wallet is linked to your Telegram ID. If you lose account access, contact support immediately. For long-term holding, we recommend withdrawing your SOL to an external wallet like Phantom.

What is price impact?

Price impact is how much your trade moves the market price. High impact means the token has low liquidity — you may receive significantly fewer tokens than quoted. BoltSnipe displays price impact before you confirm every trade.

Take Profit / Stop Loss

How to set TP/SL

After buying a token, open the Sell menu, select your position, and tap Set TP/SL. Enter a take profit price (in SOL per token). You may skip either level if you only want to set one.

How the watcher works

BoltSnipe checks the live price against your levels every 10 seconds, around the clock. When the price reaches your TP or SL, a full sell is executed automatically and you receive a Telegram notification.

How to cancel TP/SL

Go to the Sell menu, select the position, tap Set TP/SL, and type clear for either field to remove it.

What if price gaps past my level?

If price moves sharply between the 10-second checks, the bot will execute at the next available price, which may be different from your exact TP/SL level. This is standard slippage behavior on any trading platform.

Security

Private key encryption

When your wallet is created, your private key is encrypted using AES-256-GCM — the same standard used by banks and governments worldwide. The encrypted key is stored in the database. The encryption secret is held separately and is never exposed. Your unencrypted key is never stored or logged anywhere.

Non-custodial

BoltSnipe never holds your funds directly. Your SOL and tokens sit in your own Solana wallet. BoltSnipe can only act on your wallet when you initiate a trade or withdrawal. You can withdraw your funds to any external wallet at any time, no questions asked.

How to keep your Telegram account secure

Your BoltSnipe wallet is tied to your Telegram account. Enable two-step verification on Telegram to protect your account. Never share your Telegram login or verification codes with anyone. If you suspect your account is compromised, withdraw your funds to an external wallet immediately.
